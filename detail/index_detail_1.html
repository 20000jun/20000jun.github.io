Lamp Woon



A cloud-shaped light. With an interactive light using an aduino, lifting a cup and pouring the weather in the cup into the cloud produces a colored light and sound for each weather. If you pour a cup containing rain, the blue light and rain sound will be produced. If you pour a cup containing lightning, the green light and the thunder sound will be produced. If you pour a cup containing snow, the white light and sound like crunch will be produced. And when you pour all the cups together, the rainbow-colored light and the sound that birds chirping on a sunny day will be produced.
